    The 54 highest points of the Tramuntana

    1 minute read

    One of my personal “challenges” during my PhD in Mallorca is to reach the 54 highest points in Mallorca, which are higher than 1000 m. Below you can find an interactive map with the locations of all this points and you can find its name and height by clicking the icons.

    The list of the 54 highest points in Mallorca
